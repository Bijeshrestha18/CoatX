Our 4-Step Process

How We Preserve Your Fruits

1

Quality Sorting

Fruits are carefully sorted by size, ripeness, and quality to ensure only the best produce enters our production line.

2

Sanitization

Thorough washing with purified water and food-safe sanitizers to remove any impurities or contaminants.

3

Coating Application

Precise application of our plant-based coating using advanced spray technology for uniform coverage.

4

Quality Control

Rigorous quality checks and packaging under hygienic conditions ready for market distribution.
