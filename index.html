Our Process

1. Initial Preparation

Fruits are carefully pre-sorted, brushed, and thoroughly washed in purified water to remove contaminants before coating.

2. Nano-Layer Coating

A thin, edible layer of plant-based coating is applied using advanced spray technology for a uniform, protective seal.

3. Final Finish & Export Prep

Coated fruits are quickly dried and buffed to a desirable shine, then packed for immediate domestic or international shipping.

The Founders Story

From humble beginnings to revolutionizing Nepal's fruit preservation industry

Kanchan

Co-Founders & CEO, CoatX Nepal

Growing Up Among Nepal's Orchards

Growing up in the lush hills of Nepal, Kanchan always believed that fruit should be pure and untouched, straight from the farm to the table. So when she first noticed a shiny waxy layer on imported apples and oranges, she felt uneasy. Like many Nepalese consumers, she assumed it was artificial, unsafe, and something that didn’t belong on real organic fruit.

But one question kept circling in her mind: “If waxing is harmful, why is it being sold everywhere?” Her curiosity led her to dig deeper. Through her studies in agricultural science and days of reading during library shifts, she uncovered something surprising: Many waxes are naturally plant-based and they protect fruits from losing moisture further extending shelf life without affecting quality.

The "Aha!" Moment

In 2019, while comparing a waxed and unwaxed apple side-by-side, she noticed the unwaxed one shrivel quickly while the waxed one stayed fresh. That simple observation changed everything. At the same time, she saw local farmers losing up to 40% of their harvest to spoilage, while Nepal continued importing fruits from India and China. She realized a natural preservation solution could support local farmers, extend shelf life, and reduce the country’s dependence on imports. Driven by this vision, she researched for few months and came up with the solution. The result was CoatX: a gentle, plant-derived fruit protection solution that reduces waste, boosts farmers’ income, and brings fresher Nepalese fruits to the market.

Today, CoatX serves hundreds of farmers across Nepal, helping them get better prices for their produce and reducing food waste significantly. Our dream is to see Nepalese fruits on tables around the world, fresh and delicious as if just picked from our orchards.

"If we can save a single fruit, we can save a farmer’s hope."
— Kanchan
